This guide lists current payment method coverage by country for Xendit In-Person Payment Terminal.

<Info>
Availability may change based on market rollout, partner readiness, and merchant configuration. Contact [inpersonpayments@xendit.co](mailto:inpersonpayments@xendit.co) for the latest enablement status.
</Info>
