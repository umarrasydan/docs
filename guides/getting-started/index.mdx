## Popular Integration Paths

<Tabs>
<Tab title="E-commerce Store">
  <Steps>
  <Step title="Choose payment methods">
    Select from cards, e-wallets, and bank transfers that work in your target markets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate checkout">
    Use our web SDK or mobile SDKs to add payment processing to your store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle webhooks">
    Set up webhook endpoints to receive payment notifications and update order status.
  </Step>
  </Steps>
</Tab>

<Tab title="Retail POS System">
  <Steps>
  <Step title="Set up Terminal Gateway">
    Install our Terminal Gateway app or SDK on your Android/iOS devices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Terminal API">
    Connect your terminals to our API for payment processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test transactions">
    Use our sandbox environment to test all payment scenarios before going live.
  </Step>
  </Steps>
</Tab>

<Tab title="Mobile App">
  <Steps>
  <Step title="Install mobile SDK">
    Add our mobile SDK to your iOS or Android application.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Implement payment flow">
    Create payment screens using our pre-built UI components or custom implementations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process payments">
    Handle payment processing, receipts, and error scenarios in your app.
  </Step>
  </Steps>
</Tab>
</Tabs>
